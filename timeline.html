The Last Ice Age

Massive glaciers covered much of the Earth. Temperatures were colder, and large animals like mammoths roamed the frozen tundra.

Warming Climate

As the Earth warmed, ice sheets melted and sea levels rose. Forests expanded, and humans began to adapt to new environments.

The Holocene Epoch

The start of human civilization — agriculture, settlements, and the birth of history as we know it.
