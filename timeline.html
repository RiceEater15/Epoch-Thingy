Early Holocene Epoch (11,700 to 8,200 years ago)

We see the climate starting to stable more then ever, glaciers started to retreat including oceans getting warmer with coral reef starting to expand, forests and grasslands starting to be form, rivers like the amazon starting to form due to many glacial water melting. Humans enter into hunters and gathers era including agriculture with the stone age finally here. Modern species starting to take place as we can see due to the bison and brown bears and wolfs starting to evolve.

Middle Holocene Epoch (8,200 to 4,200 years ago)

Our climate is in one of the warmest phases of our epoch, we have mild humid conditons. Forests start to dominate Asia and Europe valleys with river become more fertile for farming. Wetlands and deltas started to form including very big lakes arrived. Cattle, pigs, sheep goats and chickens start to become more widespread across the world. Decline with wild megafauna.
